## Cosmological Simulations

Presentations related to numerical cosmology and galaxy formation simulations.

- **Accurate Density Field Statistics with Fast Simulations**  
  Bachelor’s thesis presentation on accuracy–cost trade-offs in N-body simulations.

- **Cosmological Simulations of Galaxies**  
  Presentation on the review paper by Feldmann & Bieri (2025) on galaxy formation in cosmological simulations. In collaboration with Raffael Jonas.
